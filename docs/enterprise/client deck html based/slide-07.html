Why 'Bill Shock' Blocks AI Scaling

Are you getting the most value from your AI spend, or just burning cash?

"Bill Shock" is the single biggest barrier to scaling AI. Without granular, real-time tracking, a single inefficient prompt or new feature can lead to a 10x cost spike before finance sees the bill a month later. This destroys budgets, kills project momentum, and makes CFOs skeptical of further AI investment.
