Eliminate Silent Failures & Protect Your Brand

Why 'Silent Failure' is Your Biggest Risk

How do you know if your agents are giving good, accurate, and on-brand answers?

"Silent failure" is the most significant risk in AI operations—where your agent confidently provides subtly wrong answers for months. This erodes user trust, corrupts downstream data, and damages your brand, all without triggering a single traditional alarm. A simple "pass/fail" check is insufficient to protect your investment.

Our Managed Service for Quality Assurance

Our AI Operations team provides multi-layered quality assurance:

  • Continuous Quality Scoring: 0-10 rubric customized to your use cases, moving beyond pass/fail
  • Proactive Drift Detection: We don't just report drift; we fix it via fine-tuning, prompt engineering, or model-switching
  • Automated Evaluation-as-a-Service: LLM-as-Judge evaluators that scale quality control for every interaction
  • Human-in-the-Loop Curation: Expert validation creating "golden sets" to ensure automated judges stay accurate
  • Cost-vs-Quality Optimization: Quarterly reports identifying "cost-per-quality-point" for data-driven model selection

Tangible Benefits & Core KPIs

  • Avg. Quality Score (Target: >8.5): Your top-line measure of user trust and brand protection, monitored in real-time
  • Quality Distribution (Target: <5% 'Poor/Fail'): Tracks consistency of user experience, eliminating the long tail of bad interactions
  • Failure Rate (Target: <2.5%): Bottom-line metric for unacceptable outputs, driven as close to zero as possible
  • Drift Detection Alerts: Your early warning system—not noisy alerts, but actionable tickets for investigation before user impact

Impact: Early drift detection prevents 90% of quality issues before they reach users, enabling prompt optimization and model selection decisions that protect your brand and user trust.
